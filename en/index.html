Rotary club Sofia Triaditsa

We are a group of friends, gathered by the common desire to do good. We take our causes to the heart and we put a lot of passion in our work achieving them. Our selfless service to the community provides us both with a goal and a velocoty. We find real joy when we provide for the others. If this calls home to you, please contact us.
