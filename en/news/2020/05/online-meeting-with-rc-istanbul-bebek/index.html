Online meeting with RC Istanbul Bebek

2020-05-12

The global pandemia may be complicated, but meetings with friends are still on the menu. And even more so when we are talking about the long-expected and desired ones! Last night’s first online meeting between Rotary club of Sofia Triaditsa and Rotary Club of İstanbul-Bebek was the real proof of that!

The atmosphere was friendly and uplifted. The decision for having online meetings on a monthly basis assures us that in the near future we will be meeting in person and will take a group photo with the Bosporus or Vitosha mountain in the background. We are excited to exchange experience in project management in both districts, to discuss current trends in our countries, to help each other overcome the consequences from the Covid-19 and over time to share funny memories and to laugh together.
