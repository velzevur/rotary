Rotaract lector: Boyana

2020-04-23

The third lector of Stay Proud Rotaractor - Webinar is Boyana Bankova!

She is a charter secretary of Interact club Sofia Vitosha NHSFB 2001/2002, a Past-President of Rotary club Sofia-Vitosha 2009/2010, a Past DRR 2010-2011, a charter member and a Past-President 2018-2019 of the second Rotary clyb in our district created by the New generations. She has been part of the commitie for the new generations for 7 years and currently is its chairman.

During the emergency situation Boyana is a dreamer, she dreams for tommorow!

The subject that she will enlighten them is: the new policies of Rotary International for Rotaract
